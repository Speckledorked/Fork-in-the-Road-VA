Our Story

Our journey began with a simple desire: to serve food we'd proudly feed our daughter. Tre brings over a decade of restaurant experience, while Christa's hospitality expertise ensures every event feels personal. Together, we're bridging the gap between Virginia's farms and your celebrations, one seasonal menu at a time.

We believe exceptional food starts with exceptional ingredients. That's why we partner with local farms within 50 miles, ensuring peak freshness and supporting our community's agricultural heritage.
