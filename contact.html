Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy requirements with advance notice. Our seasonal menus naturally offer diverse options.

Can we customize the menu?

Yes! While we have set packages, we love creating custom menus that showcase seasonal ingredients and match your vision. Custom menus may affect pricing.

Do you provide staff for events?

Yes, our packages include appropriate staffing. For buffet service, we provide setup and maintenance. Premium packages include dedicated service staff.

What about plates and utensils?

We provide high-quality compostable serviceware standard. China and glassware are available for premium events.

Is a tasting included?

Complimentary tastings are offered for weddings with 100+ guests. Other events can schedule tastings for a fee that's credited toward your booking.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your date. Cancellations made 14+ days in advance receive a full refund. Please ask about our complete policy.
