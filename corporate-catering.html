Our Corporate Catering Process

Reliable execution from first call to final cleanup. Here's how we work with corporate clients:

1

Initial Consultation

Fast turnaround: We typically respond within 24 hours and schedule consultations at your convenience.

What we discuss: Event goals, guest count, dietary restrictions, budget parameters, venue logistics, and any sustainability reporting needs.

Timeline: 30-45 minute call or in-person meeting.

2

Itemized Proposal

Procurement-friendly: Detailed line-item pricing, farm sourcing information, and clear terms make budget approval straightforward.

What's included: Custom menu, staffing plan, equipment needs, setup timeline, and sustainability metrics if requested.

Timeline: Delivered within 3-5 business days. Revisions welcome.

3

Optional Tasting

Who qualifies: Complimentary for corporate events over $5,000. Available by request for smaller events (fee credited toward booking).

What happens: Sample 4-6 dishes, meet our team, finalize any menu adjustments.

Timeline: Scheduled 2-4 weeks before your event.

4

Final Coordination

Reliability matters: We confirm final headcount, coordinate with your venue and AV team, and prepare detailed day-of logistics.

Timeline: Final count due 7 days before. Coordination call 3-5 days before.

5

Event Day Excellence

Professional execution: Early arrival, discreet setup, attentive service, thorough cleanup. Your event runs smoothly while you focus on business.

Follow-up: Post-event check-in within 48 hours to ensure satisfaction.
