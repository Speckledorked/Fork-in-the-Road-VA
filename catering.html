How It Works

From initial conversation to final cleanup, we handle every detail so you can focus on your guests. Here's our proven process for delivering exceptional events:

1

Initial Consultation

What we discuss: Your event vision, date, location, guest count, dietary restrictions, service style preferences, and budget parameters.

Timeline: 30-45 minute phone or video call, typically within 24 hours of your inquiry.

What you'll receive: Honest assessment of how we can meet your needs and preliminary ideas for your menu.

2

Custom Proposal

What we create: A detailed seasonal menu featuring dishes made with Virginia's current harvest, complete with farm sourcing information and service logistics.

Timeline: Delivered within 3-5 business days of consultation.

What's included: Itemized pricing, staffing plan, equipment needs, setup/breakdown timeline, and terms. We'll revise the proposal as needed until it's exactly right.

3

Tasting & Refinement

Who qualifies: Complimentary for weddings over 100 guests and corporate events over $5,000. Available by request for smaller events (tasting fee applied and credited toward final invoice).

What happens: Sample up to 6 dishes, meet our team, discuss final details, and make any adjustments to your menu.

Timeline: Scheduled 2-4 weeks before your event date.

4

Final Coordination

Before your event: We confirm final guest count, coordinate with your venue and other vendors, finalize timeline, and prepare detailed day-of logistics.

Timeline: Final guest count due 7 days before event. Final coordination call 3-5 days before.

What's confirmed: Setup time, service flow, dietary accommodations, equipment placement, and point of contact.

5

Event Day Excellence

What we handle: Early arrival for setup, complete meal preparation and service, attentive staff throughout your event, and thorough cleanup before departure.

Your role: Relax and enjoy your event. We've got everything covered.

After service: Follow-up within 48 hours to ensure your complete satisfaction.
