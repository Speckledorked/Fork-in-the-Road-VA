Catering Services

Bringing Virginia's farms to your celebration

Farm-to-Table Catering That Tells a Story

Every Fork in the Road event is more than just a meal – it's a celebration of Virginia agriculture. Our catering brings the same commitment to local sourcing, seasonal menus, and genuine hospitality that defines our brand. Whether you're planning a corporate gathering, wedding reception, or community festival, we'll create an experience your guests will remember.

Our Catering Services

Corporate Events

Align your company events with sustainability values while impressing clients and employees. Our ESG-friendly menus showcase local farms and seasonal ingredients, creating natural conversation starters and memorable experiences.

  • Executive lunches and dinners
  • Team building events and retreats
  • Product launches and client entertainment
  • Holiday parties and milestone celebrations
  • Farm sourcing cards for each dish
  • Sustainability reporting available

Minimum spend: $3,500 | Lead time: 2 weeks

Weddings & Private Events

Make your special day uniquely Virginia with a menu that reflects the season and supports local farms. From intimate rehearsal dinners to grand receptions, we'll craft a dining experience as memorable as your celebration.

  • Cocktail hour appetizers and stations
  • Plated dinners or buffet service
  • Late-night snack offerings
  • Custom menu development
  • Coordination with venue and planners
  • Complimentary tasting for weddings over 100 guests

Starting at: $35/person | Lead time: 3-4 weeks

Festivals & Community Events

Bring farm-fresh flavors to festivals, farmers markets, and community gatherings. Our mobile kitchen delivers high-volume service without sacrificing quality or our commitment to local sourcing.

  • À la carte service from our truck
  • Festival-friendly menu options
  • Quick service for large crowds
  • Branded presence at your event
  • Compostable packaging standard

Pricing: À la carte $14-22/item | Lead time: 2 weeks
