Service Styles

Choose the format that matches your wedding's atmosphere

Plated Dinner

Best for: Formal receptions, structured timelines, seated dinner

Multi-course meal with pre-selected entr√©e options. Guests choose their entr√©e in advance. Professional servers deliver each course. Creates an elegant, restaurant-quality experience.

Buffet Service

Best for: Casual to semi-formal receptions, varied dietary needs

Abundant spreads with multiple entr√©es, sides, and salads. Guests serve themselves or we staff the buffet line. Offers variety and accommodates different appetites and preferences.

Family-Style

Best for: Intimate celebrations, creating a communal vibe

Large platters placed on each table for guests to share. Encourages conversation and connection. Works beautifully for rustic, farm-inspired, or backyard weddings.

Chef Stations

Best for: Interactive receptions, cocktail-style events

Multiple cooking stations where chefs prepare food to order. Guests move between stations. Great for adding a unique, memorable element to your celebration.

Cocktail Reception

Best for: Shorter celebrations, budget-conscious couples

Heavy hors d'oeuvres and small plates throughout the evening. No seated dinner. Keeps guests mingling and creates a relaxed, social atmosphere.
